About Me

I am currently a Junior in the School of Information, graduating in April 2019 with a focus in UI/UX design and Japanese. I am passionate about full stack development and global technology. Going through college, I have sought to study how technology affects different societies around the world. From a small village in northern India to a small pottery town in southern Japan, I have been able to help create technology to be used different enviroments. I hope to work abroad after graduation. I spend alot of my time outside of class enjoying sports such as Archery and Badminton. I also love to sit down, listen to music, and read a good book.
